Publications

Romain Brégier, Frédéric Devernay, Laetitia Leyrit and James L. Crowley, "Symmetry Aware Evaluation of 3D Object Detection and Pose Estimation in Scenes of Many Parts in Bulk", in IEEE International Conference on Computer Vision Workshop (ICCVW), 2017. paper, supplementary material , poster.
In this paper, we propose an evaluation methodology for object detection and pose estimation that overcome limitations of existing approaches. Notably, it enables to properly deal with arbitrary rigid objects, by explicitly considering their symmetries.

R. Brégier, F. Devernay, L. Leyrit, and J. Crowley, "Defining the pose of any 3D rigid object and an associated distance", manuscript submitted for publication, 2016. article.
Journal article in which we revisit the notion of pose of a rigid object, and propose some tools to efficiently deal with it, based on mathematical proofs.
